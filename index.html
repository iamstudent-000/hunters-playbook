How to use this

1. Go to Checklist → click any row to see step-by-step "how to test this".
2. Right-click a row for quick actions: mark done, copy the payload, or open full details.
3. Or just click the ○ icon on the right of a row to mark it done — your progress is saved automatically in this browser.
4. Use Library for deep-dive guides per vulnerability type, and Reports for real disclosed write-ups.
